How to take care your dog ?

  1. Provide a Healthy Diet
    • Choose quality dog food: Select a balanced diet suitable for your dog’s age, size, and health.
    • Regular feeding schedule: Feed your dog at the same times each day.
    • Fresh water: Always have fresh water available for your dog.

  2. Exercise Regularly
    • Daily walks: Take your dog for walks to keep them active and healthy.
    • Regular feeding schedule: Feed your dog at the same times each day.
    • Fresh water: Always have fresh water available for your dog.

  3. Regular Grooming
    • Brush your dog: Regular brushing keeps your dog’s coat healthy and reduces shedding.
    • Bathing: Bathe your dog as needed using dog-friendly shampoo.
    • Nail trimming: Trim your dog’s nails to prevent discomfort and health issues.
